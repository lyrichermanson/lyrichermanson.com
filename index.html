I am a Physical Activity Specialist, competitive athlete, and online coach based out of Central Michigan — though you'll often find me on the road. The pursuit of this sport has taken me further than I ever expected, and that experience shapes everything about how I coach.

I have been through the grind of figuring it all out on my own — the training, the nutrition, the mental side of things. I know what it is like to struggle, to hit walls, and to have to rebuild. That is not a weakness. That is the work. And it is exactly why I take this so seriously.

My approach is grounded in traditional, data-driven, evidence-based training and nutrition. No gimmicks, no shortcuts. Every program is tailored towards you, your goals, your schedule, and your life — because I know firsthand that life does not slow down for your fitness goals.
